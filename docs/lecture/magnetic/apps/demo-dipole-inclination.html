Dipole Anomaly vs. Magnetic Inclination

The same buried body makes a different anomaly depending on where you are on Earth. Move it from the equator to the pole.
Total-field anomaly of a buried dipole magnetized by the ambient field. At low inclination the anomaly is a positive-negative pair; toward the pole it becomes a single symmetric high centered on the body.
