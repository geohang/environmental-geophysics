Skin Depth Calculator & Visualizer

How deep does an EM field reach? Skin depth sets the answer, and it depends on frequency and ground conductivity.
–
skin depth δ (field falls to 1/e ≈ 37%)
$\delta = \sqrt{\dfrac{2}{\omega\mu\sigma}} \approx 503\sqrt{\dfrac{\rho}{f}}$ (metres, with $\rho$ in Ω·m and $f$ in Hz). Lower frequency and more resistive ground both push the field deeper.
