Two-way time along the hyperbola: $t(x)=\dfrac{2\sqrt{d^2+(x-x_0)^2}}{v}$. Relative permittivity from velocity: $\varepsilon_r=\left(\dfrac{c}{v}\right)^2$ with $c=0.30$ m/ns. This fit assumes constant velocity, a point-like target, zero time offset, and negligible antenna separation.
